## Platform-Specific Notes

### Dzen (Яндекс Дзен)
- **Publishing Schedule**: 3-4 posts per week, optimal times: 8-10 AM, 1-3 PM, 7-9 PM MSK
- **Format**: Multimedia-friendly; includes embedded images/videos
- **SEO Strategy**: Use long-tail keywords in titles; Include topic tags
- **Engagement Goal**: Comments and shares; Encourage discussion

### Telegram
- **Publishing Schedule**: Daily posts; 2-3 long-form threads per week
- **Format**: Text-based with emoji/stickers; Pin important announcements
- **Link Strategy**: Bot links or shortened URLs in pinned message
- **Engagement Goal**: Reactions and saves; Build community

### Medium
- **Publishing Schedule**: 2 posts per week; Publish Tuesday/Thursday 9 AM PT
- **Format**: English language; Publish to "Faberlic Satire" publication if available
- **SEO Strategy**: Keyword research; Include relevant tags
- **Engagement Goal**: Follows, highlights, bookmarks; Respond to comments

### WordPress Blog
- **Publishing Schedule**: 2-3 posts per week; Publish Tuesday/Friday 10 AM PST
- **Format**: Featured image required; Internal linking mandatory
- **SEO Strategy**: Yoast optimization; Meta descriptions; Schema markup
- **Engagement Goal**: Email subscriptions; Social shares; Affiliate clicks

## Monthly Content Themes

- **Month 1-3**: Foundation building (ingredient education, myth-busting, culture satire)
- **Month 4-6**: Seasonal content (summer skincare, travel-friendly products)
- **Month 7-9**: Advanced strategies (DIY hacks, science deep-dives, influencer parodies)
- **Month 10-12**: Holiday campaigns (gift guides, limited editions, year-end roundups)

## Affiliate Integration Best Practices

1. **Authentic Recommendations**: Only promote products that fit the narrative
2. **Link Placement**: Natural integration; 2-3 affiliate links per post (depending on length)
3. **Disclosure**: Always include "This post contains affiliate links" disclaimer
4. **Tracking**: Use UTM parameters for each platform to measure ROI
5. **Variety**: Rotate through different product categories to avoid oversaturation

## Content Batching Strategy

- **Batch 1 (Week 1)**: Record/write 4 Dzen articles, 10 Telegram posts, 2 Medium pieces
- **Batch 2 (Week 2)**: Write 4 WordPress posts, 15 Telegram posts, create graphics
- **Batch 3 (Week 3)**: Repurpose content for secondary platforms; Plan seasonal content
- **Batch 4 (Week 4)**: Analytics review; Plan next month's themes; Update content calendar

## Performance Metrics to Track

For each platform:
- Page views / Impressions
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-through rate on affiliate links
- Email subscriptions (WordPress only)
- Follower growth
- Average time on page / Read time

Monthly goals:
- Dzen: 50,000+ impressions, 5-10% engagement
- Telegram: 1000+ channel members, 15%+ engagement
- Medium: 5000+ views, 500+ follows
- WordPress: 10,000+ sessions, 2-3% affiliate CTR
